Ebike trips taken on Citi Bike's 5/5/2022 NYC Ebike launch

Citi Bike launched new Ebikes on May 5, 2022 to its Citi Bike and Lyft Pink members. Explore where riders took the new Ebikes on launch day through this map.

Blue points show where riders started their trips. Bike trip data is from Citi Bike.

Click on any blue point to view the route the rider may have taken. Routes are estimated using MapBox's Directions API.
